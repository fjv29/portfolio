About Me

Hello, my Name is Francisco J Vargas, a dedicated professional with a passion for Computer Science. Throughout my career, I have honed my skills and expertise through challenging roles and impactful projects.

My journey into the world of computer science started while I was balancing a full-time job and raising three wonderful children. Determined to pursue my passion for technology, I taught myself to code during late nights and weekends, showcasing my relentless work ethic and commitment to personal growth.

With a solid foundation in in computer Coding, I have successfully honed my ability to create websites from scratch. This experience has not only strengthened my technical abilities but also taught me the importance of perseverance and adaptability in a fast-paced environment. I hold a 27% of a Codecademy Full-Stack Web Development Certificate, which has provided me with a strong theoretical framework to complement my practical skills. My dedication to continuous learning drives me to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in computer science.

As I look towards the future, my goal is to develop my skills enough to be able to contribute to larger projects. I am excited about the opportunity to work in this field and use my skills to help a wider audience. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I invite you to explore my work and connect with me for potential collaborations or discussions about web development.
